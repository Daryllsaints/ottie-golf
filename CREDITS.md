# Credits

## Sound effects

The six sound files under `public/sounds/` are derived from the following Kenney audio packs, both released under the Creative Commons CC0 1.0 public domain dedication. No attribution is required by the license, but listing the source here as a thank-you and so future maintainers know where to find replacements or related sounds.

- **Kenney Impact Sounds** (https://kenney.nl/assets/impact-sounds)
  - `swing.ogg` from `impactPunch_medium_001.ogg`
  - `cup-rattle.ogg` from `impactPlate_light_001.ogg`
  - `splash.ogg` from `impactSoft_heavy_001.ogg`

- **Kenney Interface Sounds** (https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds)
  - `sink.ogg` from `drop_001.ogg`
  - `heckle.ogg` from `error_006.ogg`
  - `tap.ogg` from `click_002.ogg`

License: CC0 1.0 Universal (https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)

## Tile art

Wang tilesets in `public/tiles/` were generated through the Pixellab MCP integration. Generation parameters and chained base_tile_ids documented in `docs/PRD.md` section 5.7.

## Sprites

Character sprites (`public/sprites/ottie-ready.png`, `ottie-swing.png`) and the tree sprite are commissioned through Pixellab for the Ottiegotchi / Ottie product line and reused here. They are project-owned assets.
